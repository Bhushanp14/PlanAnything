About PlanAnything

PlanAnything is a visual daily planner that helps you organize your tasks and track your progress across multiple plans.

With PlanAnything, you can:

  • Create multiple plans for different areas of your life
  • Add tasks with descriptions and photos
  • View your tasks on an interactive calendar
  • Track task completion with visual indicators
  • Stay organized and productive
